Recipe from Public Domain Recipes by Organization.Metropolitan Life Insurance Company

Things to Prepare...

  • 2 squares unsweetened chocolate
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2/3 cup flour
  • 3 egg, yolks, slightly beaten
  • 1 tablespoon butter, or margarine
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 baked 9-inch pie shell
  • 3 ripe bananas

Photo by Anastasia Dulgier on Unsplash

Yield: 1 pie

Directions

  1. Melt chocolate in milk in top of double boiler over boiling water, beating until blended
  2. Combine sugar, salt, and flour
  3. Stir slowly into chocolate mixture
  4. Cook, stirring constantly until thick
  5. Cook 10 minutes longer; stir occasionally
  6. Stir small amount of hot mixture into egg yolks; then pour back into remaining hot mixture while beating vigorously
  7. Cook 1 minute
  8. Add butter or margarine and vanilla
  9. Cool thoroughly
  10. Cover bottom of pie shell with small amount of cooled filling
  11. Peel bananas; slice into pie shell
  12. Cover with remaining filling
  13. Top with sweetened whipped cream and additional ripe banana slices, if desired
  14. Or, make a meringue using 3 egg whites and 6 tablespoons sugar
